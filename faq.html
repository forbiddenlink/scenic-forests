Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cabin rentals, policies, and amenities.

Booking & Reservations

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our online booking system on the Reservations page. Select your desired cabin, dates, and follow the checkout process. We'll confirm your booking via email within 24 hours.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer full refunds for cancellations made 30 days or more before check-in. Cancellations made 14-29 days before check-in receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 14 days before check-in are non-refundable.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

Yes, most cabins require a minimum 2-night stay. During peak seasons and holidays, minimum stays may be extended to 3-4 nights.

Cabin Amenities

What amenities are included in all cabins?

All cabins come equipped with full kitchens, Wi-Fi, smart TVs, heating/AC, basic toiletries, linens, and towels. Most cabins also feature hot tubs, outdoor seating areas, and gas grills.

Is Wi-Fi available in all cabins?

Yes, all our cabins feature complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi. However, due to our remote location, speeds may vary.

Are the cabins pet-friendly?

Select cabins are pet-friendly with an additional fee. Please check individual cabin listings for pet policies and contact us for details.

Check-in & Policies

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 4:00 PM, and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request.

Is smoking allowed in the cabins?

All our cabins are strictly non-smoking. Smoking is only permitted in designated outdoor areas. Violation of this policy will result in additional cleaning fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept digital payments through PayPal.
